    Psalm 53 (1–6)

    1. The fool says in his heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt, and they do abominable and evil deeds; there is no one who does good.
    2. God looks down from heaven upon the children of man to see if there are any who understand, any who seek after God.
    3. Everyone has turned away; they have all alike become corrupt. There is no one who does good, not even one.
