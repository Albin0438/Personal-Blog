From the Heart

  1. The fool says in his heart, "There is no God." They are corrupt, and they do abominable and evil deeds; there is no one who does good.
  2. God looks down from heaven upon the children of man to see if there are any who understand, any who seek after God.
  3. Everyone has turned away; they have all alike become corrupt. There is no one who does good, not even one.
  4. Will those who do evil never learn? They consume my people as they eat bread, and they never pray to God.
  5. There they are, overcome with terror, where there was nothing to fear! For God has scattered the bones of those who attacked you. You put them to shame, because God has rejected them.
  6. Oh, that salvation for Israel would come out of Zion! When God restores the fortunes of His people, Jacob will rejoice and Israel will be glad.

-Psalm 53 (1:6)
